How is this person engaged?

Each type has different tax treatment.

Full-time employee
On the company's books with a standard employment contract. Subject to PAYE and SSNIT.
Part-time employee
Regular but reduced working hours. Subject to PAYE and SSNIT.
Casual worker
Daily or short-term, less than 6 continuous months. Subject to a flat 5% withholding tax. No SSNIT.
Secondary employment
This is their second job (their primary employer is elsewhere). Subject to a flat-rate PAYE on this income.
