What's your SSNIT Employer Registration Number?

When your company registered with SSNIT, you got an Employer Registration Number. It starts with E plus 10 digits, from your SSNIT employer certificate. This number identifies your company, not individual employees. Find it on monthly SSNIT contribution statements.

What is your business's Tax Identification Number?

Issued by the Ghana Revenue Authority. You'll find it on your TIN certificate or on any tax-related document from GRA.

When does the company's contribution officially belong to the employee? (Vesting)

Choose "Immediate" if they own it right away, or select a timeline based on how long they work here.

Immediate Ownership
The company's contribution belongs to the employee the exact day it is paid. If they leave your company tomorrow, they take 100% of it with them.
All-at-Once (Cliff Vesting)
Employees get 0% of the company's money until they reach a specific milestone (e.g., working here for 2 years). Once they cross that date, they own 100% of it.
Milestone Bonus (Graded Vesting)
Employees gradually earn ownership over a few years (e.g., they own 25% after year one, 50% after year two, etc.) until they own it completely.
