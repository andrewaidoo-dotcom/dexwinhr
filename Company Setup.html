When was your business registered?

What is your Business Registration Number?

Issued by the Registrar General's Department (RGD). You'll find it on your Certificate of Incorporation or Business Registration certificate.

What is your business's Tax Identification Number (TIN)?

Issued by the Ghana Revenue Authority. You'll find it on your TIN certificate or on any tax-related document from GRA.

How is your business registered?

This refers to your business's formal registration status with the Registrar General's Department.

Is your business privately or publicly owned?

A public company has its shares listed on a stock exchange like the Ghana Stock Exchange. Most businesses are private.

How does your business run day to day?

These are standard banking compliance questions.

Does your business need an operating licence to run?

Some industries — like banking, telecoms, healthcare, education, and security — need a licence from a regulator. Most businesses don't.

What sector does your business operate in?

Pick the broadest category that fits. You'll choose a more specific industry next.

More specifically, what industry does your business operate in?

The options here update based on the sector you picked above. Choose the industry that most closely matches what your business does day-to-day.

How much money does your business make a year?

Your total revenue in a typical year, before expenses. An estimate is fine — use figures from your most recent financial year if you have them.

Where does most of the money coming into your business come from?

A standard banking compliance question. Pick the option that describes the majority of your income.

How many people are required to sign off on payments?

This is set out in your company's mandate. It tells us how many authorised people must approve each payment from your account.

How can we reach you?

Affinity uses these to send you account notifications and statements. Your business address is also verified against your Ghana Post GPS code.

Where is your business located?

Use the registered address of your business, as it appears on your business documents.

How can we contact your business?

Affinity uses your phone and email to send account notifications, security alerts, and statements. Your website helps us learn more about your business. The primary phone is required; the rest are optional but recommended.
