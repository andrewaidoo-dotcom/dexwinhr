FAQ

Questions, answered

Still curious? Email hello@dexwin.gh — we usually reply within an hour during business days.

Do you actually file SSNIT and PAYE returns on our behalf?
Yes — Dexwin submits SSNIT (by the 14th) and PAYE/NHIL/GETFund (by the 15th) directly to the SSNIT and GRA portals each month, using your company's registered credentials. Filing receipts are stored against the payroll run so you have an audit trail.
How long does it take to set up?
Most teams are running their first payroll within a week. Import a CSV of employees (or upload your last payslip batch and we'll structure it), connect your bank, and verify your SSNIT and GRA credentials. Our implementation team walks you through it on a 45-minute call.
Can employees see their payslips and request leave themselves?
Yes. Every employee gets a self-service portal (web + WhatsApp) where they can view payslips, download tax certificates, request leave, and update bank details. Managers approve from the same surface — no email back-and-forth.
Which banks do you support for salary transfers?
Direct integrations with GCB, Ecobank, Stanbic, Absa, Zenith and Fidelity. For other banks, Dexwin generates a payment file in the format your bank accepts. Mobile money payouts via MTN, Telecel, and AirtelTigo are supported on all plans.
What about employees we pay in USD or GBP?
Dexwin handles multi-currency salaries. We use the Bank of Ghana mid-rate on payroll day to compute PAYE and SSNIT contributions in Cedi while paying out in the contracted currency.
Is our data secure?
All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, stored in audited data centres, and access-controlled by role. We're registered with the Data Protection Commission of Ghana and undergo annual third-party security reviews.
