Employee
On your company's books with a salary. Full-time, part-time, casual, or secondary employment. PAYE and SSNIT apply.
Independent Contractor
Engaged for a specific service. Invoices the company; not on the payroll. Withholding tax. No PAYE, no SSNIT.
National Service Personnel (NSS)
On national service, posted to your company by the NSS. Receives an allowance — no PAYE, no SSNIT.
Intern
Student or recent graduate gaining workplace experience. Receives a stipend or is unpaid. Subject to PAYE if the stipend exceeds the tax-free threshold; generally no SSNIT.
